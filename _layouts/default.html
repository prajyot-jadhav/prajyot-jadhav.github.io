Prajyot Jadhav

I’m a Research Engineer in Robotics at the Autonomous and Kooperative Systems Lab at the University of Tartu, Estonia, advised by Prof. Arun Kumar Singh.

I’ve previously worked with JdeRobot through Google Summer of Code, collaborating with Prof. José María Cañas Plaza and Prof. Pedro Arias Pérez on the open-source software Robotics Academy. During my pre-final year summer, I worked on adaptive control strategies for quadrotors under the supervision of Prof. Poramate Manoonpong.

I hold a Bachelor’s in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), India. During my time there, I was part of IvLabs, the AI and Robotics lab of VNIT, where I developed many of my research interests under the guidance of Prof. Shital Chiddarwar.
